Guacamole

Description

A delicious Mexican dip made of primarily of mashed avocadoes, that can be served as a topping for other mexican dishes, or on it's own with tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 6 Avocadoes
  • 1 Red Onion, finely diced
  • 1 Jalapeno, finely chopped
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 1 Large Lime
  • 1/2 Tbsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Salt

Steps

  1. Scoop avocadoes into a large mixing bowl and mash until they have a smooth uniform consistency (or until desired chunkiness).
  2. Squeeze in the juice of the lime.
  3. Add in the rest of the ingredients and stir.
  4. Enjoy!
